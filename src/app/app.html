The estate

A quieter kind of luxury.

Not marble lobbies or velvet ropes. Stone warmed by the afternoon sun. Olive trees moving in the breeze. A long table, an open door, and nowhere else you need to be.

Finca Es Castell brings the character of rural Mallorca into every part of the stay — unhurried, grounded and close to the landscape.

Open skies.

The estate carries its history in the details: worn steps, timber doors, green shutters and courtyards where the boundary between indoors and outdoors almost disappears.

Living the estate

Experiences that
nourish the soul.

Days here follow the landscape: mountain paths in the morning, ancient olive trees in the afternoon and a sky full of stars after dark.

01

Hiking

Guided routes through the Serra de Tramuntana, a UNESCO World Heritage landscape.

02

Century-old olive trees

Walk among sculptural trunks shaped by time, alongside carob, fig and seasonal fruit trees.

03

Wellness

Yoga, meditation and massages with essential oils, arranged around the rhythm of your stay.

04

Stargazing

Far from city light, the night sky above the estate becomes a spectacle of its own.

05

Local wildlife

Look out for mountain goats and the eagles, falcons and vultures that cross the open sky.

06

Wine tasting

Discover Mallorcan wines and the distinctive aromas of the Serra in a tasting arranged for you.
