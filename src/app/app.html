The estate

A quieter kind of luxury.

Not marble lobbies or velvet ropes. Stone warmed by the afternoon sun. Olive trees moving in the breeze. A long table, an open door, and nowhere else you need to be.

Finca Es Castell brings the character of rural Mallorca into every part of the stay — unhurried, grounded and close to the landscape.

Made by time

Old walls.
Open skies.

The estate carries its history in the details: worn steps, timber doors, green shutters and courtyards where the boundary between indoors and outdoors almost disappears.
